Hiking

Hiking is definitely my favorite outdoor activity. I love to hike in the snow, in the rain, or just in plain sunny weather. My favorite part is climbing tall mountains and taking beautiful pictures of the scenery. I also love to just enjoy the peacefulness that comes along with being outdoors. Learning Languages

One of my odd hobbies is learning new languages for fun. While this is a hard tasks, it is very rewarding for me to accomplish This. The two languages that I have practiced the most have been Japanese and Spanish. One of my favorite parts about learning these languages are that they have enabled me to understand cultures that I would have normally been oblivious to.
